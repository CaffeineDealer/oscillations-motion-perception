# Oscillations - Motion Perception

Time-frequency analysis of oscillatory power across 32 channels spanning MT to MST, comparing responses to complex vs. translational motion stimuli.

## Observations
 
- Both conditions show a transient power increase in ~25–50 Hz, peaking ~125–250 msec after stimulus onset, across nearly all channels.
- A secondary peak frequency appears for some channels at the same frequency band but at ~375 msec.
- Low-frequency band (~5–10 Hz) diverges by channel during the same time window: power increase in higher channels.
- Complex and translation conditions are similar in overall timing and frequency structure; differences are in magnitude/exact peak per channel rather than a distinct qualitative pattern.
